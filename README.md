# 📊 E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing 5,000 e-commerce 
transactions across 2023–2025, built to uncover sales trends, 
regional performance, category profitability, and discount impact.

---

## 🔍 Business Questions Answered

- Which product categories generate the most revenue?
- How do monthly sales trend across 2023–2025?
- Which regions are most profitable?
- Does discounting help or hurt profit margins?

---

## 📁 Dashboard Features

- **Sales by Category** — Ranked bar chart across 10 categories
- **Monthly Sales Trend** — Line chart showing revenue over time
- **Sales vs Profit by Region** — Comparative analysis across 4 regions
- **Avg Discount vs Avg Profit** — Profitability impact of discounting
- **Interactive Slicers** — Filter entire dashboard by Region and Year
- **KPI Summary** — Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Orders

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Dashboards)
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) — Jupyter Notebook
---

## 📌 Key Insights

-  Home Decor leads with ₦57.2M, followed closely by Furniture (₦56.6M) and Clothing (₦55M). Groceries recorded the lowest revenue at ₦47.9M.
-  Sales peaked in May 2025 at ₦26M. October 2025 shows low numbers but that's simply because the data cuts off early in that month — not a real decline.
-  The North region leads in total revenue (₦143.6M) but the East region has the highest profit margin at 15.1%. All four regions are remarkably close in margins — between 14.8% and 15.1%.
-   Electronics has the lowest average discount (9.5%) and the highest average profit (₦17,038), while Toys has the highest discount (10.6%) and lower average profit (₦16,709). This suggests aggressive discounting slightly reduces profit margins.
